Carrot Cake Recipe

A carrot cake is made primarily of eggs, flour, minced carrots and topped with a delicious chocolate fudge.

It is a brazilian delicacy and there are two types that are very popular: the first one uses raw carrots, the second adds cooked carrots to the finished mix.

Batter

  • half a spoon of oil
  • four eggs
  • three medium carrots
  • two and a half cups of wheat flour
  • two spoons of sugar

Topping

  • one spoon of butter
  • one cup of sugar
  • three spoons of powdered chocolate
  • one cup of milk
