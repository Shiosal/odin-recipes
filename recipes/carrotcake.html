Carrot Cake Recipe

A carrot cake is made primarily of eggs, flour, minced carrots and topped with a delicious chocolate fudge.

It is a brazilian delicacy and there are two types that are very popular: the first one uses raw carrots, the second adds cooked carrots to the finished mix.

Batter

  • half a spoon of oil
  • four eggs
  • three medium carrots
  • two and a half cups of wheat flour
  • two spoons of sugar

Topping

  • one spoon of butter
  • one cup of sugar
  • three spoons of powdered chocolate
  • one cup of milk

Cooking Directions

  1. put every ingredient of the batter onto a blender
  2. bake into a preheated oven at 180 degrees celsius for approximately 40 minutes
  3. put butter; powdered chocolate; sugar; milk on a big bowl
  4. mix it well, while applying heat, until obtaining a consistent texture
  5. place the topping on top of the baked dough
