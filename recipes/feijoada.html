Feijoada Recipe

Feijoada is a popular brazilian dish, originally from the north of Portugal, it is based on black beans.

In Brazil, it is made from a mix of black beans with various cuts of meat from cows and pork. It is usually served with farofa, white rice and couve.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of black beans
  • 100g of dried meat
  • 100g of pork ribs
  • 150g of portuguese sausages
  • 2 big onions

Cooking Directions

  1. let the meat rest for up to 36 hours, submerged in water
  2. cook all the cuts gradually, starting from the hardest
  3. mix it with raw beans
  4. season with salt, pepper and onions
  5. prepare the side dishes: couve, farofa, torresmo, arroz branco, etc
