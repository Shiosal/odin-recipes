Pumpkin Soup Recipe

Cooking ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 cups of pumpkin puree
  • 4 cups of chicken stock
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • half cup heavy whipping cream

Directions

  1. heat everything up
  2. make sure it is plenty soft
  3. season well
  4. eat as much as you want
